Sanji's Fried Rice for Gin

Description

Feed the hungry, no matter who they are: that's the job of a cook. Sanji serves Gin a plate of steaming hot fried rice that he whipped up quick. Sanji's dedication to feeding the hungry brings tears to the eyes of this demon man.

Ingredients:

  • 360g rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g corned beef
  • 1/4 onion
  • 4 brown mushrooms
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Dash black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • Green onion (as desired)

Steps:

  1. Finely chop the onion. Take off the mushroom caps and thinly slice. Cut a small amount of green onion.
  2. Put the vegetable oil in the frying pan, put in half the onion and all of the mushrooms and stir fry. Add the corned beef and stir fry.
  3. Put the ingredients from step 2 aside. Put some vegetable oil in the pan and add beaten eggs (A). Before the egg cooks, add the rice and quickly stir fry. Mix all together. Add salt and pepper. Add the rest of the onion. Add soy sauce (B). Mix a little.
  4. After putting on a plate, sprinkle the green onion on top.
  5. Enjoy!
