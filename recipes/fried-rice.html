Fantastic & easy to cook Japanese Fried Rice - Yakimeshi

Ingredients:
  • 1 tablespoon vegeatble oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 small leek or onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small carrot (finely chopped)
  • 300 grams 1-2 days old cooked Japanese rice that was kept in the fridge
  • 1 cup lettuce (shredded)
  • 2 large eggs (whisked)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • salt and ground white pepper (to taste)
Instructions:
  1. In a wok or large pan over high heat, add oil and garlic and cook for 30 seconds
  2. Add onions and carrot and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until vegetables are soft but still yielding a crunch.
  3. Add cooked rice and lettuce and stir until the ingredients are mixed in with the rice.
  4. Push rice to one side and add eggs. Continuously stir and break the eggs with your spatula until they're almost fully cooked.
  5. Mix the eggs with the fried rice and add soy sauce.
  6. Season with salt and pepper and stir until the sauce and seasonings are evenly spread through the fried rice.
  7. Turn the heat off and serve.
